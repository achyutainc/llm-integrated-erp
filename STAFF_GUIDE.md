Welcome to the Local ERP AI system! This guide helps you use the AI tools to manage the store efficiently.

The AI (located in your Dashboard) is connected to our inventory and order system. It acts as a helpful assistant.

### 1. Inventory & Expiry Management
*   **Check Stock:** "Do we have milk?" or "How many apples are left?"
*   **Expiry Alerts:** "Check for expiring products."
    *   *Tip:* The AI will highlight items expiring within 7 days.
*   **Locate Items:** "Where is the Basmati Rice?" (If location data is added).

### 2. Marketing Assistant
*   **Draft Posts:** "Draft a Facebook post about our fresh Ontario Apples."
*   **Instagram Captions:** "Write an Instagram caption for our Butter Chicken special."
*   **Schedule Posts:** (Coming Soon) "Schedule this post for tomorrow at 5 PM."

### 3. Order Management
*   **Restock:** "Create an order for 20 units of Milk."
*   **Customer Orders:** "Create a takeout order for Butter Chicken and Naan."
